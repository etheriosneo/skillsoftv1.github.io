About Skill Soft

What we do!
Skill Soft provides an all-in-one solution for all your job requiremnts, a job portal for daily wage workers. You have a job post it on our portal! You need a job ? come register with us. No hidden charges!

We have registrations for both Job Provider and Job Seeker. Register with us and make your hiring process safe, secure and reliable. Job Providers registered with us can post jobs according to their requirement.

The Job Seekers registered with us can view and express their interest in doing the jobs according to their interest, that are posted by yhe Job Providers.
